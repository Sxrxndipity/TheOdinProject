Fettuccine

Creamy gourmet pasta

Ingredients
  • Homemade Pasta
  • Cream
  • Premium Butter
  • S&P oregano and basil
  • Chicken breast and some parmesano reggiano

Steps
  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Melt the butter and the cream, add the seasoning and stir
  3. Fry the chicken after seasoning
  4. Toss the pasta in the sauce add the cheese
  5. Serve the pasta with the chicken on top
